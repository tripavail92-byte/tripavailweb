 TOUR CREATION FLOW (7 Steps)
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│           TOUR OPERATOR - TOUR CREATION                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Tour Operator Dashboard > "Create New Tour"
        │
        ▼
STEP 1: BASICS
  • Tour Name
  • Tour Type (City, Adventure, Cultural, Food, Nature)
  • Duration (hours/days)
  • Difficulty Level
  • Languages offered
        │
        ▼
STEP 2: ITINERARY
  • Day-by-day breakdown
  • For each day:
    - Morning activities
    - Afternoon activities
    - Evening activities
    - Meals included
    - Accommodation (for multi-day)
  • Add/remove days dynamically
        │
        ▼
STEP 3: PRICING
  • Per person price
  • Group pricing (optional)
  • Min participants
  • Max participants
  • Child pricing
  • Early bird discounts
        │
        ▼
STEP 4: MEDIA
  • Tour photos
  • Videos
  • 360° images (optional)
        │
        ▼
STEP 5: POLICIES
  • What to bring
  • Fitness requirements
  • Age restrictions
  • Cancellation policy
        │
        ▼
STEP 6: CALENDAR
  • Set tour dates
  • Available slots per date
  • Blackout dates
        │
        ▼
STEP 7: CONFIRMATION
  • Review & Publish
